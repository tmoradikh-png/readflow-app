## Information We Collect

Documents and document text:

- When you import a supported PDF or Word `.docx` file, readFlow may process the
  file to create a phone-friendly reading view.
- If the document needs server extraction, OCR, AI answers, or Cloud AI voice,
  the document file, extracted text, page images, selected text, prompts, and
  related metadata may be sent to readFlow backend services.

AI and voice requests:

- If you use AI summaries, explanations, simplified text, key points, Q&A, or
  Cloud AI voice, we may process the selected document text, prompt, language,
  voice choice, and generated response or audio information.

Usage and entitlement information:

- We collect information needed to enforce plan limits, such as monthly counts
  for document imports, OCR pages, AI actions, and Cloud AI voice characters.
- We use an app user id to associate usage and subscription entitlement with the
  app installation or purchase account.
- If subscriptions are enabled, Google Play, Apple App Store, and RevenueCat may
  provide purchase, subscription, entitlement, renewal, cancellation, and
  restore information.

Technical information:

- We may collect technical logs needed for security, debugging, rate limiting,
  abuse prevention, and service reliability, such as request time, endpoint,
  response status, app version, device platform, IP address, and error messages.

Information stored on your device:

- Your local library, bookmarks, reading progress, preferences, downloaded rF AI
  voice model, generated audio cache, and local document cache may be stored on
  your device.

Support information:

- If you contact us, we collect the email address, message content, attachments,
  and any other information you choose to provide.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Import, extract, reflow, OCR, and display documents.
- Provide read-aloud, rF AI, Cloud AI voice, and AI reading help.
- Save bookmarks, reading progress, and preferences.
- Enforce free and paid plan limits.
- Process subscriptions, restore purchases, and manage entitlements.
- Improve quality, reliability, security, and abuse prevention.
- Respond to support requests.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## Service Providers

We may use service providers to operate readFlow, including:

- Render or similar hosting providers for backend services.
- OpenAI or similar AI providers for AI answers and Cloud AI voice when you use
  those features.
- RevenueCat, Google Play, and Apple App Store for subscriptions, purchases, and
  entitlements.
- Email/support tools if you contact support.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your private documents
for advertising.

## Local rF AI

rF AI voice runs on supported phones after an optional model download. It uses
your phone's CPU, storage, and battery. Local rF AI narration is designed to
avoid per-character cloud voice cost. Some related settings, generated audio
clips, and model files may remain on your device until cleared by the app,
operating system, or uninstall process.

## Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described
above, unless a longer period is required by law, security, fraud prevention,
accounting, or dispute handling.

On-device documents and cached files can usually be removed by deleting books in
the app or uninstalling the app. Backend usage counters and subscription records
may be retained for billing, abuse prevention, and legal reasons. If you want us
to delete information associated with your use of readFlow, contact
`support@urmiaworks.com`.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Use manual reading instead of AI or voice features.
- Use Phone voice or rF AI where available instead of Cloud AI voice.
- Delete books from your local library.
- Cancel subscriptions through the app store account settings for the platform
  where you purchased them.
- Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion where applicable.

Some cloud features cannot work without sending document text or related data to
backend services. If you do not want a document processed by cloud services, do
not use OCR, AI answers, or Cloud AI voice for that document.

## Security

We use HTTPS for data in transit where supported. We keep API keys and payment
secrets on the backend, not inside the mobile app. No method of transmission or
storage is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children

readFlow is not designed for children. Do not use readFlow if you are under the
minimum age required by your country or platform rules. If we learn that we have
collected personal information from a child without appropriate consent, we will
take reasonable steps to delete it.

## AI Limitations

AI, OCR, extraction, and voice features are assistive. They may be incomplete,
incorrect, or unsuitable for some documents or languages. You should verify
important information against the original document and professional sources.

## International Processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. Those
countries may have different data protection laws.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be
posted with a new effective date. Continued use of readFlow after an update
means you accept the updated policy where allowed by law.
